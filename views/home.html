About Me

Lifetime Memories began out of my passion for photography.  My mission is to bring your images to life and preserve Lifetime Memories through creative and unique designs with quality and integrity at reasonable rates, while maintaining a high, moral work ethic based on Christian values.

I reside in McKinney, TX with my husband who is the inspiration for a lot of my work.  I graduated from Dallas Baptist University with an MBA in Marketing.  I shoot exclusively in digital and am aslo proficient in Adobe Photoshop.  I serve clients in the DFW area, but also love traveling so please don't hesitate to ask me about destination details.

I absolutely enjoy what I do, and I do it with lots of creativity.  Your life's captured moments are sure to create memories that will last a lifetime.
